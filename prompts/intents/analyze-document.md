# Intent: Analyze a Document

## Problem Domain

**User Goal:** Extract or analyze concepts from a specific, known document.

## Recognition

- "Extract concepts from [document]"
- "What concepts are in [document]?"
- "Analyze [document]"
- "Show me the key ideas in [document]"
- "List concepts from [document]"

## Maps To

**Skill:** [document-analysis](../skills/document-analysis.md)

## Expected Outcome

- List of primary concepts in the document
- Technical terms and terminology
- Related concepts and categories
- Document summary
