# Intent: Explore a Category

## Problem Domain

**User Goal:** Understand what concepts appear in a specific domain/category.

## Recognition

- "What concepts are in [category]?"
- "What topics are covered in [domain]?"
- "Explore [category] concepts"
- "What ideas appear in software engineering documents?"

## Maps To

**Primary Skill:** [category-exploration](../skills/category-exploration.md)

**Supporting Skills:**
- [library-discovery](../skills/library-discovery.md) — Understand category scope and document coverage
- [concept-exploration](../skills/concept-exploration.md) — Drill into specific concepts discovered

## Expected Outcome

- Key concepts in the category
- Concept frequency across documents
- Related categories
- Representative documents
- Deeper exploration of notable concepts
